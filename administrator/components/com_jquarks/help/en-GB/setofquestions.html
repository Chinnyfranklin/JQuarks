Sets of Questions

Sets of questions are groups of questions. They are a way to classify your questions before affecting them to a quiz Two type of sets are availables :

  • Custom Sets
  • Random Sets

Custom Sets

In custom sets you may choose the questions you wish to affect to your set. In this kind of set questions may be of a same or different category. Affecting a question is easy :

  • Use the filters to search your question's list.
  • Add/Remove a question by a single click.

Random Sets

In random sets you choose a category of questions from where you would like to pick up a specified number of questions An equal number of questions will be then picked up randomly from the provided category.
