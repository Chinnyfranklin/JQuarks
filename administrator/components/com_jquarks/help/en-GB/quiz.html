Quiz

Quizzes are to be presented to your users. They are selections of sets of questions. Quizzes are of two types :

  • Public Quiz
  • Private Quiz

Public Quiz

By defining a quiz as public, any visitor will be granted access to the quiz.

Private Quiz

By defining a quiz as private, it become only available for the users you choose from the registered user's list. You may notify a user of the availability of such quiz by sending him an email notification after affecting him to the quiz.

Quiz's Configuration

Once a Quiz is created you can modify its configuration :

Paginate

You may choose to make your quiz paginated or on one single page. If you choose to paginate a quiz you may specify the number of questions to show on each page. Sliding effect is available in switching from one page to another.

Publication

You can schedule the availability of your quizzes by setting starting and ending publication dates
